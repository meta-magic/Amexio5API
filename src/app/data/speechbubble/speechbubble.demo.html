The Listbox is WAI ARIA-accessible through the Tab key.And for implementing the keyboard navigation for its component role and is tested against the popular screen readers.

Role:Checkbox-The Checkbox role defines menu as its type.

Role:Listbox-The listbox role defines list as its type.It consist of aria-selected and aria-label

Role:Option-The option role defines listitem as its type.
