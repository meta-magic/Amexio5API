The Grid is WAI ARIA-accessible through the Tab key.And the best practices for implementing the keyboard navigation for its component role and is tested against the popular screen readers.

Role:Listbox-The Listbox role defines list as its type.

Role:Option-The option role defines listitem as its type.

Role:Checkbox-The checkbox role defines its type.It consist of aria-hidden,aria-checked and aria-labelledby.

Role:Grid-The grid role defines datatable as its type.
